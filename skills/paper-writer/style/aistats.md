# Style: AISTATS

Position: theory-leaning ML venue, between ICML and JMLR.

## Section template

- **8 pp main at submission** (9 for camera-ready); references,
  reproducibility checklist, and appendix excluded. Appendix in the same
  PDF; US Letter; fonts must match the style file.
- Reproducibility checklist is part of the submission — fill it honestly,
  don't stub it.
- Same skeleton as ICML/NeurIPS but theory section is mandatory and gets
  more weight

## Tone

Hybrid between ICML and JMLR. Active voice ok; mathematical statements
are the spine.

## Reviewer expectations

- Theoretical contribution must be identifiable. Pure-engineering papers
  are desk-rejected.
- Will probe assumptions aggressively but accept asymptotic results.
- Will check connections to classical statistics — flag any direct
  analogue with M-estimation, empirical process theory, semiparametric
  efficiency, etc.

## Distinct dos

- Reference classical statistical analogue (M-estimation, semiparametrics,
  empirical process, sieves, U-statistics)
- Statement of result must be precise: "We prove ... under (A1)–(A3)"
- Identifiability and regularity stated explicitly

## Don'ts

- No "we observe empirically that..." as the main contribution
- No vague theorem statements
- No bulleted Related Work or Discussion — paragraphs with explicit Δ;
  lists only in the §F slots of `prose_hygiene.md`
